Welcome to my website!

I'm a M.Sc. student in Sytems and Automation at UFSC (Federal University of Santa Catarina), Brazil.

My research interests span from combinatorial optimization to deep learning. Right now, I'm focusing on MINLP, MILP, and applications. I'm currently working on MINLP for oil production optimization, and writing my master's thesis on deep-learning-based heristics for MILP, both under the supervision of professor Eduardo Camponogara. Previously, I have worked with deep learning for medical imaging (under the supervision of prof. Danilo Silva), physics-informed learning and implicit models (B.Sc. thesis), and HPO/AutoML (during an internship at Fraunhofer IPT).

The best way to contact me is by email: mpacheco.bruno@gmail.com
